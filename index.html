Common Questions

Everything You
Want to Know

Are you licensed and insured in Florida? +
Yes. Fully licensed and insured in the state of Florida. Your home and our crew are protected on every job. We'll share documentation before starting any project.
Is the estimate really free? No strings attached? +
Completely free. We come to your home, walk through the project, and give you a detailed written quote — no charge, no obligation. Review it on your own time and decide.
How soon can you start my project? +
Most projects start within 1-2 weeks of booking. Once you approve the estimate, we lock in your start date and stick to it. No waiting around for months.
Will the final price match the estimate? +
Yes. Our estimates are detailed and thorough. The quote we give you is the price you pay. If any scope changes come up, we discuss it with you first — nothing gets added without your approval.
What areas do you serve? +
Sarasota, Lakewood Ranch, Bradenton, and the surrounding Sarasota County area. Drop your ZIP code in the form and we'll confirm right away.
Do I need to be home during the work? +
Not necessarily. As long as we have access, you can carry on with your day. We send progress photos and check-ins throughout. Most clients come home to a finished, spotless space.
What paint and materials do you use? +
We use Sherwin-Williams paints — premium quality that holds color in Florida's climate. For flooring: hardwood, LVP, and tile using top-grade materials. We never cut corners on materials.
How long does a typical job take? +
Most jobs are completed in days, not weeks. A full interior repaint: 2-3 days. Cabinet painting: 3-5 days. Bathroom remodel: scoped individually. We give realistic timelines and hit them.
